MochiNav V5.0.0

  • Connection:
    1. Added a feature to adjust the auto-off time for BLE when there is no connection with the phone: 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, or 30 minutes.
  • Expressions:

  • Sound:
    1. Added a master ON/OFF switch for all device sounds, and separate ON/OFF for emoji sounds.
    2. The ESP32-C3 Super Mini has only 4MB of flash memory, so only 102 expressions + 2 intro sounds are stored internally. Emoji sounds will be stored on an SD card.
    3. SD card must be formatted in FAT32. Emoji sounds are in Wav 16-bit 22050Hz Mono format, numbered from 1 to 102.

  • Clock:
    1. Added lunar calendar display in the clock interface.
    2. Added weather forecast and current temperature synced from the Chronos app.
  • Performance Optimization:
    The program has been optimized for stability and faster performance.
  • Quick Shortcuts: Added quick shortcuts in the emoji interface:
    1. If a button is present, press once or press "Find Watch" to switch to the Clock screen.
    2. Press the button twice quickly to toggle random or sequential animations.
    3. Press the button three times quickly to toggle sound.
    4. Press the button four times quickly to toggle the delay time when playing random animations.

Program Activation Guide

1

Connect to WiFi

The ESP32 will display the WiFi name and IP address on the OLED screen as shown below. Connect to the WiFi network without a password.

2

Access the IP Address in Your Browser

Open your browser and enter the IP address shown on the OLED screen.

192.168.4.1
3

Enter WiFi Information and Activate

You will be asked to enter:

  • Select WiFi: Click Scan WiFi to find available networks
  • Password: Enter your WiFi password
  • Activation Key: Enter the activation key you were provided

After entering, click Activate.

4

After Clicking "Activate"

When you click Activate, the system will start connecting to WiFi and verifying the entered key. In the browser, you will see a notification like this:

At this point, the ESP32 will connect to WiFi and verify the entered key.

Wrong Key Case

If the entered key is incorrect, the ESP32 will restart and prompt you to repeat the WiFi Connection step (Step 1).

Correct Key Case

If the entered key is correct, the system will complete the verification process and proceed to the main program. The screen will display a success message.

The ESP32 will automatically restart and switch to main operating mode.
